Step 3. Start trading. Buy long when price is above 50 days Simple Moving Average and short when price is below 50 days Simple Moving Average

Everything here is simple – you have to predict whether the exchange rate will rise or fall within 15 minute after opening a trade. Remember that you can make any forecast with this strategy – it doesn’t matter whether you choose UP or DOWN.

Let’s say that you’ve chosen Long

  • If the chart actually goes up, $1.82 will be returned to your account, having made 82 cents in profit

  • If the chart goes the other way (down), then your trade was unsuccessful

    This means that you need to raise your next trade to 0.2 and change the direction of the chart again. So, if your last choice was Short, now you need to choose Long.

  • If the chart didn’t go the way you wanted again, then you lose this trade

    But don’t worry. You have nothing to worry about because with this strategy you always make up for all losses. To recoup your losses and make a profit, increase the trade amount to $0.4 (without changing the trade time) and choose Short.
